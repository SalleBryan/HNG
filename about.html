About Me

A reflective look at my journey, goals, and aspirations.

Bio

I'm Salle Bryan, a frontend developer with a passion for creating accessible and delightful user experiences. My journey into web development started with a curiosity about how websites work, which quickly evolved into a deep appreciation for languages like HTML, CSS, JS and libraries like React and Bootstrap.

Over the years, I've learned that the best interfaces are those that work for everyone, regardless of their abilities or the devices they use. This philosophy drives my work and inspires me to continuously improve my craft.

Goals in This Program

  • Master advanced frontend techniques
  • Deepen understanding of progressive enhancement and resilient web design
  • Build a portfolio of projects that demonstrate best practices
  • Learn to balance aesthetic design with functional accessibility
  • Contribute to open-source accessibility tools and documentation

Areas of Low Confidence

  • Accessibility
  • Naming Things
  • Browser APIs and Compatibility
  • Advanced CSS Grid layouts with dynamic content
  • Performance optimization for large-scale applications

Extra Thoughts

Accessibility isn't just an extra feature, it's an essential part of good design. As I keep learning and growing in web development, I'm dedicated to promoting inclusive practices and making sure my work helps create a better digital world. Everyone deserves an easy and enjoyable experience, and I aim to make that happen through careful design and development.
